BACKGROUND

I'm a student at the University of Science and Technology of Southern Philippines pursuing a degree in information technology. I'd like to learn more about my chosen undergrad degree because I have always thought that this course can help me reach a successful life. Although there are times that life seems unfair, but we really have to keep moving forward and fight our way up to the top.

My parents were incredibly strict, but that didn't stop me from living my life how I wanted. I consider life to be an art form, and I am the creator of my own life. Despite my parents' extensive lists of "does and don'ts," I strive to keep it as colorful and upbeat as I can. I am a passionate individual who is naturally curious. No matter what is happening, I want to keep a smile on my face because it inspires me to push through. I enjoy spending time with the people I care about, yet I actually prefer to spend my time alone. I can reflect, unwind, and have a better understanding of how to manage life thanks to it.
